Stumptown Coffee Roasters

When you think of local coffee, you think of Stumptown Coffee Roasters. With multiple locations across Portland, New York, Los Angeles, and even Japan, Stumptown has a little something for everybody. They offer the most inclusive coffee menus, from sugar-free syrups to milk alternatives.

Coava Coffee Roasters

Coava was built on a foundation of respect and honesty, when it’s founder, Matt Higgins, sought out a sustainable coffee roasting business. He found farmers who were committed to producing quality crops in under-sourced regions. These partnerships garnered him the reputation he has today.

Water Avenue Coffee Roasters

This local Portland coffee roaster is all about supporting the local community and embracing the city of Portland. Despite being surrounded by a city of great coffee shops and roasters, Water Avenue stands out by hiring the best baristas and professionals who want to grow.

Sterling Coffee Roasters

Adam McGovern and Aric Miller, the founders of Sterling Coffee Roasters, have always made their customers feel at home. When asked, “What makes Portland coffee special?”, McGovern responds: “The secret is service experience, first and foremost.”
